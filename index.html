Direct Auction Bidding Details

  • Access to Global Auctions: We give you direct access to major live auto auctions in Japan, the UK, and worldwide.
  • Verified Quality: Detailed auction sheets and inspection reports are checked thoroughly before bidding.
  • Best Market Prices: No hidden dealer margins. You pay the actual auction price plus a fixed service fee.
  • Complete Transparency: Live bidding updates and step-by-step guidance.

Custom Clearance & Logistics (Hambantota Port)

  • Hambantota Port Operations: Complete handling of all customs clearance and logistics directly at the Hambantota Port.
  • Hassle-Free Documentation: We manage all necessary import permits, tax documentation, and customs declarations.
  • Secure Port Logistics: Safe offloading, vehicle inspection, and secure clearance from the port authority.

Doorstep Delivery Services (Island-wide)

  • Flatbed & Carrier Transport: We use premium flatbed recovery trucks or car carriers to transport your vehicle safely, ensuring zero mileage added.
  • Comprehensive Insurance: Full transit insurance coverage from the port/yard until the keys are handed over to you.
  • Island-wide Coverage: Safe and secure delivery to your doorstep, anywhere in Sri Lanka.
  • Final Inspection & Detailing: Prior to delivery, the vehicle undergoes a final multi-point inspection and a professional clean-up.

Premium Vehicle Sourcing Services

If you are looking for a specific, rare, or luxury vehicle, we tap into our extensive global network of elite dealerships and private collections to locate the exact make, model, and specification you desire. Every sourced vehicle undergoes strict physical inspections, odometer verifications, and history checks before we manage the entire purchasing, negotiation, and international shipping process on your behalf to deliver a hassle-free experience.
